Scholarships

National Cyber Scholarship Foundation

The National Cyber Scholarship Foundation provides scholarships and training programs to identify and nurture cybersecurity talent. It supports students and professionals in advancing their cybersecurity skills and careers.

SANS CyberTalent Immersion Academy

The SANS CyberTalent Immersion Academy offers scholarships and training for individuals looking to start or advance their careers in cybersecurity. It provides intensive, hands-on training programs.

(ISC)² Scholarships

(ISC)² offers various scholarships for undergraduate and graduate students pursuing degrees in cybersecurity. These scholarships aim to support and encourage the next generation of cybersecurity professionals.

CyberCorps: Scholarship for Service

CyberCorps: Scholarship for Service (SFS) provides scholarships to students in cybersecurity-related fields. In return, scholarship recipients agree to work for a government agency after graduation.
