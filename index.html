Resources

CyberStart

CyberStart offers engaging, gamified cybersecurity training and challenges for individuals and educational institutions. It's designed to inspire and develop the next generation of cybersecurity professionals.

Skills for All

Skills for All by Cisco provides free, self-paced courses covering various aspects of cybersecurity, from fundamentals to advanced topics. The platform aims to make cybersecurity education accessible to everyone.

CryptoHack

CryptoHack offers interactive courses focused on cryptography. Through hands-on challenges, learners can deepen their understanding of cryptographic principles and practices.

National Cyber Scholarship Foundation

The National Cyber Scholarship Foundation provides scholarships and training programs to identify and nurture cybersecurity talent. It supports students and professionals in advancing their cybersecurity skills and careers.
